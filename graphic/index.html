Adobe Illustrator

Adobe Illustrator is a vector graphics editor and design program developed and marketed by Adobe Inc. Originally designed for the Apple Macintosh, development of Adobe Illustrator began in 1985. Along with Creative Cloud, Illustrator CC was released

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Photoshop is a raster graphics editor developed and published by Adobe Inc. for Windows and macOS. It was originally created in 1988 by Thomas and John Knoll. Since then, the software has become the industry standard not only in raster graphics editing, but in digital art as a whole.

Participation Guidelines

Maximum no. of teams from a school – 2
Maximum no. of participants in a team – 2
Time Allotted (Finals) – 120 minutes
Software Allowed – Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator
Submission file format - .psd, .ai &
.jpg/.png
Finals Date: 02/12/2020

General Guidelines

Use of other software will lead to disqualification. The composition can be completely self-designed. Images taken from the Internet are allowed but some editing has to be done. All the images taken from the Internet have to be submitted else will lead to negative marking.

Final Round

The topic will be given on the spot. The students will have to create a minimalistic poster from scratch and are free to present his/her artwork along with the school name and participant's’ details. The students are allowed to use internet for images, but it’s not necessary.

Judgement criteria

• originality
• creativity
• technical skills
• interpretation
• graphic design
